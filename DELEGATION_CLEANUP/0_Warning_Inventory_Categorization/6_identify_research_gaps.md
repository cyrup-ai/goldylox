# Task 6: Identify Remaining Research Gaps

## Description
Analyze the completed warning categorization and classification to identify research gaps and specific investigation needs for subsequent milestones.

## Objective
Provide clear research agenda for other milestones by identifying areas where additional investigation is needed for confident warning resolution.

## Dependencies
- Task 5: Create Systematic Warning Database (must be completed first)

## Success Criteria
1. Complete inventory of research gaps identified from warning analysis
2. Specific investigation tasks defined for each gap area
3. Priority ranking of research needs based on warning impact
4. Resource requirements estimated for each research area
5. Assignment of research tasks to appropriate milestones
6. Clear success criteria defined for closing each research gap

## Implementation Steps
1. Review warning database for low-confidence classifications
2. Identify patterns where evidence was insufficient for confident classification
3. Analyze complex architectural areas needing deeper investigation:
   - MESI protocol communication patterns
   - ML feature extraction pipeline integration
   - Statistics collection and telemetry integration
   - Background worker and maintenance task integration
4. Define specific research questions and investigation methods
5. Assign research tasks to appropriate milestone teams
6. Create research success criteria and evidence requirements

## Deliverables
- `research_gaps_inventory.md` - Complete list of identified research needs
- `complex_integration_research.md` - Deep-dive research needs for sophisticated systems
- `milestone_research_assignments.md` - Assignment of research tasks to milestones
- `research_success_criteria.md` - Evidence requirements for closing gaps
- `resource_requirements.md` - Estimated effort and tools needed for research

## Notes
- Focus on high-impact areas affecting many warnings
- Prioritize research that can resolve multiple warnings simultaneously
- Consider dependency chains where one research result affects multiple areas
- Plan for iterative research where initial findings may reveal additional gaps
